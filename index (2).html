// Dispute resolution
Community-powered arbitration

Fair. Decentralized. Incentivized.

When sender and recipient disagree, the community steps in. Jurors stake ALGO to vote, and correct voters earn a share of the dispute fee.

1
Either party opens a dispute, paying a 0.5 ALGO fee to prevent spam
2
Verified community jurors review evidence submitted by both parties
3
Jurors stake ALGO and cast a vote within the 72-hour window
4
Majority wins; minority stakes are redistributed to correct voters
